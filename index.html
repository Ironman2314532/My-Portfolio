Hey, My name is Thira Patel

I am a Robotics Engineer interested in developing Automation systems, Robot Manipulation, Motion Planning and Software-Hardware Integration. Apart from this I am a self taught blogger and creative writer. I also crochet!

Let's dive in

About Me I was introduced to Robotics during my school years, when I joined an educational club. I made my first robot using a Lego Mindstorms kit in that club, after which there was no looking back. Robotics to me is a field of endless possibilities. My vision is to be a part of creating robotic systems that have a positive impact on the world. Trying to explore a variety of sub-fields within robotics, I have taken up projects in Hardware Integration, Robot Manipulation, Localization and Mapping, Motion Planning, Controls, Machine Learning as well as Computer Vision. My background in Mechanical Engineering has made me proficient in CAD modelling as well.

Projects This section discusses my projects.

Motion Planning for a Mobile Manipulation Robot Pushing a Cart

Implemented A* (baseline), Dynamic Multi-Heuristic A*, ADA*, and Bi2RRT* for a mobile robot with a nonholonomic base. Evaluated algorithm performance in a 2D dynamic environment, showcasing advanced planning techniques.

Details

Teleoperation of Aerial Robots using an Augmented Reality Virtual Surrogate

Designed an AR interface using Unity and AR Foundations to control a drone via a virtual surrogate. Conducted user study showing a 95% preference for the surrogate interface over direct drone teleoperation.

Details

Controller Design for Crazyflie 2.0 Aerial Robot

Sliding mode controller designed for the CrazyFlie 2.0 drone to track desired trajectories. Implemented the controller in a simulation model of the drone in Gazebo with parameter tuning.
